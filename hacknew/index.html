ABOUT HACKOVERFLOW

HackOverflow is a 48 hours hackathon which aims to provide hours of ideation and innovation. It is an event for hackers to challenge their technical boundaries by learning new technologies, developing innovative projects and procuring new-age solutions. This event intends to bring together like minded hackers from various colleges and universities for an amazing technical journey consisting of pre-hack webinars and workshops. The theme for this Hack is Ukraine-Russia war and is primarily based on 6 tracks namely Logistics, Defense, Fintech, Healthcare, Telecom and Media & Open Innovation. The teams will be divided into groups of 4. Domain experts from each track will judge and will provide guidance to the hackers to improve their solutions. The hackers will be awarded with special cash prizes and lots more

FAQ

+

Who can participate in the event?

The event is open to all coding enthusiasts not only from VIT, but also from other colleges!

+

Where to register for the event?

Event registration for everyone will happen on Devfolio,but VITians also have to register in VTOP(VIT's student portal)

+

How many rounds are there in the hackthon?

There are 2 rounds in the hackathon Ideation and Implementation round

+

Where will the hackathon be hosted?

The hackathon is based on the DevFolio Platform. It will be a completely online event for all external students(i.e participants who are from outside VIT) and a hybrid event (i.e participants can come to an assigned classroom to collaborate together or can work together online) for students of VIT Vellore.

+

How many people need to register for the event?

You can participate as a single participant or as a part of a team with a maximum of 4 participants.

+

Is there a specific coding language required?

No, we do not have any restrictions as far as coding language is concerned.

+

Will there be prizes given to the winners?

There are quite a few exciting prizes on the table for exceptional performers.

+

What is the last date for registering the event?

Midnight of 3rd January 2023,Thursday.
